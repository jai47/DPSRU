Welcome to the DPSRU Alumni Forum

The DPSRU Alumni Association maintains and enhances a highly engaged, vibrant community of alumni and friends worldwide. It helps connect alumni to the University and each other through a wealth of community activities, continuing education programs, online services, and events around the world.

DPSRU alumni forum (DAF) is a group of graduates from college of Pharmacy, DIPSAR and DPSRU who have come together to maintain a connection with their alma mater and with one another. This association often provide networking opportunities, host events, and raise funds for the institution. It also serves as a way for alumni to give back to their institution and support current students. Joining DAF is a great way to stay connected to your alma mater and build relationships with other alumni.


It may be thought that alumni gifts are only made by wealthy and accomplished individuals who have had time to accumulate wealth and establish themselves professionally. However, DAF is not just non-profit organizations that rely solely on donations from past students. It also offers a variety of options.


1) Networking

2) Career building

3) Giving back to the Society

Joining the alumni forum of DPSRU can offer a wide range of benefits to alumni of the college. These benefits include opportunities for networking, career advancement, and lifelong learning.


One of the main benefits of joining the alumni forum is the opportunity to network with other alumni of DPSRU. This can be incredibly valuable, as alumni can help each other with career opportunities, job referrals, and other professional development opportunities. Additionally, alumni can connect with one another to share ideas, resources, and best practices in their respective fields.


Another benefit of joining the alumni forum is the opportunity to advance one's career. Many alumni forums offer career services, such as resume review, interview coaching, and job search assistance. These services can be incredibly valuable for alumni who are looking to take the next step in their careers.


Lifelong learning is another benefit of joining the alumni forum. Many alumni forums offer opportunities for alumni to continue their education, such as workshops, webinars, and seminars. These opportunities can help alumni stay current in their field and continue to grow professionally. In addition to these benefits, joining the alumni forum can also be a great way to stay connected with the college. Alumni can participate in events, reunions, and other activities that help to keep them connected to their alma mater.


In conclusion, joining the alumni forum of DPSRU can offer a wide range of benefits to alumni, including opportunities for networking, career advancement, and lifelong learning. So, it is highly recommended to join the alumni forum ofDPSRU for the betterment of one's professional and personal life.
